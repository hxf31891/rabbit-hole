# 🌸 Sakura Viewing Guide JP26'

## 🌸 Best Early Sakura (March 28 – April 3)

### Tokyo
**Peak:** ~Mar 26 – Apr 3  

Likely **perfect timing** if you arrive soon after starting the trip.

**Best spots**
- Ueno Park
- Meguro River
- Shinjuku Gyoen

---

### Kyoto
**Peak:** ~Mar 30 – Apr 7  

You’ll likely hit **full bloom or very close**.

**Best spots**
- Philosopher’s Path
- Maruyama Park
- Arashiyama (near bamboo forest)

---

### Nara
**Peak:** ~Mar 30 – Apr 1  

Nara Park becomes **one of the most surreal sakura scenes in Japan**.

---

### Hiroshima
**Peak:** ~Mar 29 – Apr 6  

Peace Memorial Park and the **castle moat sakura** are spectacular.

---

# 🌸 Mid-Trip Sakura (Early – Mid April)

### Kanazawa
**Peak:** ~Apr 2 – Apr 8  

Kenroku-en becomes **one of Japan’s top sakura gardens**.

---

### Hakone / Fuji Area
**Peak:** ~Early April  

Lake Ashi views with **sakura and Mt. Fuji**.

---

### Sendai
**Peak:** ~Apr 5 – Apr 10  

Best around **Aoba Castle ruins and riverbanks**.

---

# 🌸 Late Sakura (Mid – Late April)

These will likely bloom **after you start but before the trip ends if you move north slowly.**

### Aomori
**Peak:** ~Apr 18 – Apr 22  

Hirosaki Castle is **one of the best sakura sites in Japan**.

---

### Hakodate
**Peak:** Late April  

The **Goryokaku star-shaped fort** fills with cherry blossoms.

---

# 🌸 Where Sakura Fits Your Route Best

Your itinerary naturally intersects **three peak zones**.

| Region | Peak Timing | Key Stops |
|------|------|------|
| Late March | Tokyo / Kyoto / Nara | 🌸 Perfect timing |
| Early April | Kanazawa / Hakone | 🌸 Very good |
| Early–Mid April | Sendai / Tohoku | 🌸 Possible depending on pace |

Northern **Tohoku and Hokkaido bloom later**, so if you go there first you’ll miss sakura there.

---

# 🌸 Top Sakura Locations Already On Your Route

These are **10/10 viewing spots**.

- Philosopher’s Path (Kyoto)
- Maruyama Park (Kyoto)
- Nara Park
- Kenroku-en Garden (Kanazawa)
- Peace Memorial Park (Hiroshima)
- Meguro River (Tokyo)

---

# 🌸 Secret Sakura Stops Near Your Route

These are **insanely beautiful but less crowded**.

### Yoshino Mountain (near Nara)
30,000 cherry trees covering an entire mountainside.

---

### Miharu Takizakura (Fukushima)
A **1,000-year-old “waterfall cherry tree.”**

---

### Hirosaki Castle (Aomori)
The castle moat fills with fallen petals, creating **pink “sakura snow.”**
